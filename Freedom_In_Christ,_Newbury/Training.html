Equipping Church leaders and individuals with an effective discipleship strategy

 
 
 
 







IMPORTANT NOTE: The FICM Newbury Team offer support free, but to run these courses for you we would ask your church to purchase their own copy of the training DVDs, from F.I.C.M. UK direct. It is essential that your church owns their own copy, so you can replicate the training in-house after we have initially supported you. 


‘Making Fruitful Disciples’ Course

This course teaches the foundational principals on which the F.I.C. discipleship course is based upon. This course is aimed at those looking to run the F.I.C. Discipleship course. 


Session 1 =  Introduction

                    Principle 1: Disciples know Jesus

                    Principle 2: Disciples know who they are


Session 2 =  Principle 3: Whole of reality

                    FIC Discipleship resources

                    FIC for young people


Session 3 =  Principle 4: Who is responsible for what?

                    Using the steps to Freedom In Christ


Session 4 =  Principle 5: Secret of ongoing transformation

                    Getting Started


Session 5 =  Principle 6 Part A: Resolving deeper issues

                    Principle 6 Part B: Resolving corporate issues


‘Helping Others Find Freedom In Christ’ Course

This course is for anyone who is likely to be involved in running the F.I.C. discipleship course, and is recommended training for those who will be leading people through the Steps To Freedom In Christ.


Session 1 = Introducing the F.I.C. approach

                   Introducing the Steps To Freedom In Christ


Session 2 = The Freedom Appointment


Session 3 = Step1: Counterfeit Vs Real

                   Step 2: Deception vs Truth


Session 4 = Step 3: Bitterness Vs Forgiveness

                   Step 4: rebellion Vs Submission

                   Step 5: Pride Vs humility


Session 5 = Step 6: Bondage Vs Freedom

                   Step 7: Curses Vs Blessings

                   Stronghold Busting


Session 6 = Deeper Issues

The FIC Newbury Team

Most of our courses are led by: Mark Broadhurst (Freedom In Christ  Newbury Team Leader)


Mark Broadhurst served for seven years as a Trustee with Freedom In Christ Ministries UK, and throughout that time was on their teaching team and served as their regional representative for the Newbury area. This coupled with his local knowledge of Newbury and years of serving with C.T.N.A. (Christians Together in the Newbury Area) ideally place him to speak at a local level here in Newbury on how F.I.C.M. UK can support your church in Newbury to establish an effective discipleship strategy.  Mark, and other FIC Newbury Team members, are happy to run the above two training courses for teams (using DVD resources) within any fellowship in the Newbury area. This will equip your team to form your own ‘Freedom Team’ in your Church to run the ‘Freedom In Christ’ discipleship courses and lead others through the Steps To Freedom In Christ. We aim to support you to establish your own ‘Freedom Team’ (to run your own courses) in your Church, so that you have an effective discipleship strategy that naturally lives as part of the life of your fellowship.
