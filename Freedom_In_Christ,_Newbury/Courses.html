WOULD YOU LIKE US TO RUN A COURSE FOR YOU!?...

We are happy to support anyone in seeking to setup Freedom In Christ courses, or indeed run them for you.

IMPORTANT NOTE: The FICM Newbury Team offer support free, but to run these courses for you we would ask your church to purchase their own copy of the course DVD(s), from F.I.C.M. UK direct. It is essential that your church owns their own copy, so you can replicate the courses in-house after we have initially supported you.

Our long term aim and hope would be to support you in establishing your own team so you are equipped to run courses yourself. If this is not possible for you now please still get in touch as we would you to be able to run the course(s) rather than you miss out for lack of a team at present to run them.

A 6 week course, to help Christians understand God’s grace and be motivated by God’s love alone (not by guilt, shame or fear) so that they go on to bear ‘much fruit’. It provides powerful, practical ways to submit to God, identify faulty thinking and renew the mind.

Freed To Lead


A brand NEW discipleship course for leaders, this teaches effective identity-based leadership. Participants on the new course will learn how to lead from their identity in Christ with authenticity and integrity, and become the sort of people that others love to follow. They will learn how to avoid drivenness and burnout, survive personal attacks, use conflict positively and the course also includes a special set of ‘Steps To Freedom For Leaders’.
