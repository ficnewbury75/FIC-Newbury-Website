Equipping Church leaders and individuals with an effective discipleship strategy

 
 
 
 







This presentation is Ideal for Church leaders, ministers, or home group leaders, in fact  anyone who has responsibility for discipleship and wants to be more effective in their ministry. It is also a great introduction to Freedom In Christ for anyone who wants to know about the ministry and its available resources.


The two hour session happens  regularly over the UK, and is led by members of the FICM UK teaching team.


We at FIC Newbury Team would be happy to come chat to you about this, and take time to explore with you what discipleship already looks like in your place of worship in Newbury and what it could potentially look like. It is our desire to help you form and establish a strategy for effective church-wide discipleship in your church here in Newbury. We believe such a strategy is effective when the discipleship is church-wide and that discipleship is identity-based discipleship, we invite you to explore with us what that means and looks like for your church.
