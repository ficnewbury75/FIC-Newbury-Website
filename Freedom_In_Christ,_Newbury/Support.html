Equipping Church leaders and individuals with an effective discipleship strategy

 
 
 
 

Support for those who have been emotionally and spiritually wounded...

“Heart for Truth” is a Christian ministry aimed at equipping the church to support those who have experienced trauma, abuse and emotional pain.

“Breakthrough” is a Christian-based charity training and supporting people to minister to those experiencing severe

emotional and spiritual distress, and promoting emotional and spiritual wholeness across the Body of Christ.


MINISTRY: Offering hope for healing and freedom to those wounded emotionally and spiritually

TRAINING: Equipping people to minister to others experiencing emotional and spiritual distress

STANDARDS: Raising standards and providing accreditation for Inner Healing ministries and ministers.

 
 

Our vision: To see every Church in Newbury established with an effective discipleship strategy, which teaches the freedom principals and is identity-based so that every Christian knows how to grow and teach others to grow to be fruitful disciples.
