Equipping Church leaders and individuals with an effective discipleship strategy

 
 
 
 
 

We are a small team of Christians who are responsible for teaching ‘Freedom In Christ’ discipleship courses in our local Church, as part of a discipleship strategy, and we support and encourage others to replicate this effective model.





We support and encourage others to form an effective church-wide discipleship strategy by

[1] raising awareness of the resources made available through ‘Freedom In Christ Ministries UK’ (a discipleship resource ministry);

[2] sharing information about what an effective discipleship strategy can look like so that Churches and individuals are well equipped to effectively disciple and care for those the Lord brings them into contact with;

[3] equipping the church to restore the wounded by signposting Churches to other organisations such as ‘Heart For Truth’ and ‘Breakthrough’.

What we do
