Equipping Church leaders and individuals with an effective discipleship strategy

 
 
 
 
 


The ‘Freedom In Christ Newbury Team’ exists to equip Church leaders and individuals in Newbury with an effective Church-wide discipleship strategy, and support existing ‘Freedom Teams’ in Newbury. We are a local group of people who use the Freedom In Christ approach in our own Church fellowships to support effective discipleship, and want to support not only one another but anyone who seeks to use this approach in their own Church.


The ‘Freedom In Christ Newbury Team’ not only supports those who are already involved in teaching these freedom principals (in Newbury) but is also here to support anyone who wants to find out more, or help you as you look to start using it in your own Church in Newbury. We want Church leaders to understand how this material can play an  important part in their identity-based discipleship strategy. We exist to help you setup your own ‘Freedom Team’ in your church (if you live in Newbury) and we can then provide ongoing support to you as you become part of the growing ‘FIC Newbury Team’.
