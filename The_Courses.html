Setting Your Marriage Free

A course for all married couples. The F.I.C.Newbury team is currently seeing two couples trained as trainers in this course, so we hope from March 2013 to be able to offer these Steps to any married couple in Newbury who is seeking to restore a marriage or make a good marriage even better as this course will benefit all husbands and wives.


Step 1: God’s Priority for your marriage

Step 2: Breaking cycles of abuse

Step 3: Balance, rights and responsibilities

Step 4: Break sexual bondage

Step 5: Releasing old hurts

Step 6: Unmask Satan’s deceptions

Step 7: Christian marriage (renewing of vows)




Setting Your Ministry (Church) Free

These are corporate steps to be taken by the board of a ministry / leadership of a Church. To be most effective we recommend that ‘Setting Your Ministry Free’ should be led by someone from the F.I.C. team / someone outside the ministry which is being taken through the Steps.


Step 1: Our Church’s strengths

Step 2: Our Church’s weaknesses

Step 3: Memories

Step 4: Corporate sins

Step 5: Attacks of spiritual enemies

Step 6: Prayer action plan

Step 7: Leadership strategy
