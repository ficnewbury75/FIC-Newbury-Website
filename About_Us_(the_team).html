About us (the team)

 
 


The ‘Freedom In Christ, Newbury’ team is made up of local Christians who oversee this ministry in their local Church in Newbury, Berkshire, UK.




The purpose of the team is to network and share fellowship in order to support this ministry across the Churches in Newbury rather than one fellowship taking the journey alone. This we can do in many ways, such as providing more ‘encouragers’ to make more freedom appointments available.


We hope to create more opportunities for many more people to go through the ‘Freedom In Christ’ course, by sharing information on when these courses are running. It is our desire to serve and connect people not only to see individuals set free, but see whole Churches set free, marriages set free, and our community set free in Christ.
